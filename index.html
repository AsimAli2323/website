Asim Ali

Political researcher and columnist writing on democracy, nationalism, political economy and popular culture in South Asia.

I write on democratic transformation, party politics, caste mobilisation, majoritarian nationalism and cultural politics in contemporary India. My essays have appeared in The New York Times, The Guardian, The Diplomat, Frontline, The Telegraph, The Hindu and The Times of India.

  • Democracy Disfigured — Jun 2025, The Diplomat
  • No Muslim, No Hindutva — Sept 2025, Frontline
